5.3 - 4 Rules of Derivatives Power rule, product rule, quotient rule, chain rule

Important Equations for this Topic

Power Rule

`y=x^n -> (dy)/(dx)=nx^(n-1)`

Chain Rule

`y=g(f(x))` where `u=f(x) -> (dy)/(dx)=(dy)/(du)*(du)/(dx)` or `(dy)/(dx) = g'(u) * f'(x)`

Product Rule

`f(x)` or `y=ab -> (dy)/(dx)=a(db)/(dx)+b(da)/(dx)` or `f'(x)=b'a+a'b`

Quotient Rule

`f(x)` or `y=a/b -> (dy)/(dx)=(b(da)/(dx)-a(db)/(dx))/b^2` or `f'(x)=(a'b-b'a)/b^2`
