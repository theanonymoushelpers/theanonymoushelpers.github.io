3.1 - Volume and Surface Area

Important Equations for this Topic

Volume of a Right Pyramid

`Volume = 1/3Ah` (A = base area, h = height)

Volume of a Right Cone

`Volume = 1/3pir^2h` (r = radius, h = height)

Volume of a Sphere

`Volume = 4/3pir^3` (r = radius)

Surface Area of a Sphere

`SA = 4pir^2` (r = radius)

Surface Area of Curved Part of Cone

`SA = pirl` (r = radius, l = slant height)

For any triangle:

`a/(sin(A))=b/(sin(B))=c/(sin(C))`

`Area = 1/2ab * sin(C)`

3.6 - Trigonometric Identities sin^2x+cos^2x=1, double angle identities

Important Equations for this Topic

Pythagorean Identity

`sin^2(theta)+cos^2(theta)=1`

Double Angle Identities

`sin(2theta)=2sin(theta)cos(theta)`

`cos(2theta)=cos^2(theta)-sin^2(theta)`

`cos(2theta)=2cos^2(theta)-1`

`cos(2theta)=1-2sin^2(theta)`

3.9 - Reciprocal Trigonometric Identities (HL)

Important Equations for this Topic

Reciprocal Pythagorean Identities

`1+tan^2(theta)=sec^2(theta)`

`1+cot^2(theta)=cosec^2(theta)`

Reciprocal Trigonometric Identities

`sec(theta)=1/(cos(theta))`

`cosec(theta)=1/(sin(theta))`

3.10 - Compound Angle Identities (HL)

Important Equations for this Topic

`sin(A+-B)=sinAcosB+-cosAsinB`

`cos(A+-B)=cosAcosB-+sinAsinB`

`tan(A+-B)=(tanA+-tanB)/(1-+tanAtanB)`

`tan(2theta)=(2tan(theta))/(1-tan^2(theta))`
